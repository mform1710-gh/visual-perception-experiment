Research Focus

My work combines architectural designs, human perception and memory, and their social effects. I am particularly interested in:

  • Housing design for deconstructions and renovations
  • Visual perception of spatial experience and memory
  • Design of semi-public / semi-private zones and their social effects
  • Comparative analysis of urban housing tenures and asset polarization

Tea rooms in the Garden
2026 · Community space design for eldery

Community spaces for eldery are designed on the ground floor in an apartment complex. We wanted to propose new type of community spaces which are scattered semi-private spaces, professionally serviced but still personal spaces for residents.
